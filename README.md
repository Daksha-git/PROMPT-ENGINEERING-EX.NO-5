# EXP 5: COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS OF DIFFERENT TYPES OF PROMPTING PATTERNS AND EXPLAIN WITH VARIOUS TEST SCENARIOS

## Aim
To test and compare how different prompting patterns respond to various prompts such as naïve prompts (broad or unstructured) and basic prompts (clear, detailed, and refined) across multiple scenarios. Analyze the quality, accuracy, and depth of the generated responses.

---

# AI Tool Used
- ChatGPT

---

# Introduction

Prompting is the method of giving instructions to an AI model in order to generate responses. The quality of AI-generated output depends heavily on how the prompt is written.

## Types of Prompts

### 1. Naïve Prompt
A naïve prompt is simple, broad, or unclear. It usually contains minimal instructions and lacks context.

#### Example:
```text
Write about Artificial Intelligence.
```

### 2. Basic Prompt
A basic prompt is refined, clear, detailed, and structured. It provides sufficient instructions and context to guide the AI model.

#### Example:
```text
Write a 200-word explanation about Artificial Intelligence including its applications, advantages, and disadvantages in simple language.
```

---

# Objective of the Experiment

- To compare naïve prompts and basic prompts.
- To analyze how prompt clarity affects AI responses.
- To evaluate responses based on:
  - Quality
  - Accuracy
  - Depth
- To identify which prompt style produces better outputs.

---

## Naïve Prompt
```text

```

### Response
A boy went into a forest and found a magical stone. The stone granted him powers, and he became famous. Everyone admired him, and he lived happily ever after.

---

## Basic Prompt
```text
Write a short creative story of about 150 words about a young boy who discovers a magical stone in a forest. Include emotions, challenges, and a meaningful ending.
```

### Response
Ravi was a curious young boy who loved exploring the forest near his village. One evening, he discovered a glowing blue stone beneath an old tree. When he touched it, the stone gave him the ability to heal injured plants and animals.

Excited by his new power, Ravi began helping the forest. However, a greedy hunter tried to steal the magical stone for money. Ravi protected the stone and realized that true power should be used to help others, not for selfish purposes.

In the end, the forest became greener and more beautiful because of Ravi’s kindness. The magical stone slowly lost its glow, but Ravi understood that the real magic was compassion and courage.

|---|---|---|

2. Private Cloud
3. Hybrid Cloud

### Advantages
- Cost reduction
- Scalability
- Easy access
- Data backup and recovery

### Applications
- Google Drive
- Microsoft Azure
- Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Cloud computing is widely used in businesses, education, healthcare, and entertainment industries.

# Scenario 4 – Advice / Recommendation

## Naïve Prompt
```text

```

### Response
Study regularly and avoid distractions.

---

## Basic Prompt


These methods improve concentration, memory retention, and overall academic performance.

# Scenario 5 – Educational Explanation

## Naïve Prompt
```text
Explain photosynthesis.
```

### Response
Photosynthesis is the process by which plants make food.

---

## Basic Prompt
```text
Explain the process of photosynthesis for school students including the chemical equation and importance.
```

### Response
Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants prepare food using sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide.

The process mainly occurs in the leaves containing chlorophyll.

### Chemical Equation

```text
6CO2 + 6H2O → C6H12O6 + 6O2
```

### Importance
- Produces oxygen
- Provides food for plants
- Maintains balance of carbon dioxide and oxygen in nature

Photosynthesis is essential for life on Earth.

## Analysis

| Parameter | Naïve Prompt | Basic Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| Quality | Very brief | Clear and educational |
| Accuracy | Correct | More informative |
| Depth | Minimal | Detailed |

# Observations

1. Basic prompts consistently produced better responses than naïve prompts.
2. Clear instructions improved response quality and relevance.
3. Structured prompts generated deeper and more accurate answers.
4. Naïve prompts sometimes worked for simple questions but lacked detail.
5. Including context, word limits, and specific requirements improved AI output significantly.

---

# Findings

- Prompt clarity directly affects the quality of AI-generated responses.
- Detailed prompts reduce ambiguity and improve response accuracy.
- Structured prompts help ChatGPT understand the expected output format.
- Basic prompts are more suitable for educational, professional, and technical tasks.

---

# Conclusion

This experiment demonstrates that prompt engineering plays an important role in improving AI-generated responses. Basic prompts, which are clear and structured, consistently provide more accurate, detailed, and meaningful outputs compared to naïve prompts. While naïve prompts may work for simple tasks, refined prompts are highly effective for generating high-quality responses in academic, creative, and professional applications.

---

# Result

The prompts for the above scenarios were executed successfully using ChatGPT. The comparative analysis showed that basic prompts generated responses with better quality, higher accuracy, and greater depth compared to naïve prompts.
